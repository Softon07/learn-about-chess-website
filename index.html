about chess

A strategy game with a long history

Chess is one of the oldest and most popular strategy games in the world. Their roots date back centuries B.C., and their influence on the development of the mind and strategic thinking is invaluable. Chess is a logical game that requires analysis, planning and anticipation of the moves of the of the opponent.

Strengthens logical thinking skills

Chess is a game that not only provides entertainment, but also strengthens logical thinking skills thinking. During the game you have to analyze situations, anticipate your opponent's moves, plan a strategy and make decisions based on available information.

A game that improves concentration and memory

Regularly playing chess develops the ability to concentrate, remember patterns and motifs, as well as improves short-term and long-term memory. These are skills that can benefit many areas of life, from studying and working to solving everyday problems.
